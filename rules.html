State 1535 Rules

Our constitution for maintaining peace and cooperation across all alliances

General Rules

Fundamental laws governing daily interactions

  • NAP Protection: All main alliances and 1 academy per alliance are protected under our Non-Aggression Pact
  • Attack Limits: Maximum 3 hits or 1 rally against non-NAP cities
  • Tag Manipulation: No dropping alliance tags to attack NAP-protected players
  • Power Gaming: Strictly no poaching members or inflating power for ranking advantages
  • Resource Safety: No attacks on resource tiles, banners, or Headquarters
  • Conduct: Bullying and toxic behavior will not be tolerated
These rules apply 24/7 outside of officially declared Kill Events

Kill Event Rules

Modified regulations during competitive events

  • Resource Tiles: May be attacked during Kill Events
  • Safe Zones: No city attacks allowed in safe zones
  • Participation: Must leave alliance territory to participate
  • Facility Battles: No city hits during Sunfire Castle or Facility fights
  • Strategic Targets: Banners and HQs remain off-limits
  • Facility Tactics: No blocking/circling facilities with banners
  • Stacking: No stacking banners on facilities
  • Coordination: No solo attacks - must coordinate with alliance

Stronghold Rotation

Fair distribution of territory and resources

  • Supreme Rotation: Alternates between 1st and 2nd ranked alliances
  • Normal Rotation: Shared between 3rd and 4th ranked alliances
  • Top 3 Benefit: Top 3 alliances receive free Stronghold
  • FFA Stronghold: Last Stronghold is free-for-all
  • Fortress Allocation: All alliances guaranteed 1 Fortress
  • Additional Fortresses: Remaining are FFA (limit 1 per alliance)
Rotation schedules reset each season and are announced in Discord

Conflict Resolution System

We prefer diplomacy, but here's our escalation framework:

First Offense

24-hour resource compensation to victim + warning

Repeat Offense

48-hour NAP suspension + public apology

Severe Violation

State-wide KoS (Kill on Sight) status for duration of event
